Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information – Username you provide when uploading data and, if you choose to log in via e-mail or Twitter/X, your e-mail address and Twitter ID.
  • Content – CSV draft data you upload, your votes, and versus match selections.
  • Technical – IP address, browser user-agent, and session cookies used for authentication and rate-limiting.

2. How We Use Information

  • Operate, maintain, and improve the Best Ball Voting service.
  • Publicly display drafts, votes, leaderboards, and related statistics.
  • Send critical notices regarding your account or uploaded data.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information. Publicly visible information is limited to your username and any data you choose to upload. We may share aggregated, anonymized statistics.

4. Cookies

Session cookies keep you logged in. They are not used for advertising or tracking across other sites.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

Uploaded data is kept indefinitely unless you request its removal. E-mail aidanhall21@gmail.com to delete your data.

6. Security

We use reasonable safeguards to protect your data but cannot guarantee absolute security.
